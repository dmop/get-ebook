Ebooks microservice

    Only for the epubr.club site
    Search for the book you want on the /search/:name route
    Pass the url of your book to return the download link on the /ebook/:url* route
    Search for the books recently added on the /news route

Example usage:

https://get-ebook.herokuapp.com/search/cronicas de duna
